music app project

developed an android music application using java in android studio, focusing on both functionality and user experience. the app allows users to play, pause, browse, and manage music tracks through a clean and intuitive interface.

the design process followed ui/ux design principles, including visual heirarchy, consistency, accessibility, and ease of navigation. low-fidelity and high-fidelity prototypes were first created in adobe xd to plan layout, user flows, and interactions before implementation.
